Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect: username, display name, login ID (email/phone), bio, profession, and an optional profile picture. All data is stored in Google Sheets.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for providing the Jh Godown service: authentication, profile display, post attribution, and feed functionality. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

File Data

Files you encode are compressed and converted to text before storage. The encoded text is stored across Google Sheets. We cannot view your original files as they are encoded. NSFW content should be marked appropriately.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage to store your session token and preferences (theme, bookmarks). No tracking cookies are used.

IndexedDB

We use IndexedDB for offline caching, bookmarks, and personal analytics. This data stays on your device.

Data Retention

Posts and user data are retained until manually deleted by the user or an administrator. You can delete your own posts at any time.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact the administrator through the platform.
